Media Monitoring

PressPulp know's how time consuming it can be to keep on top of your media coverage. You might not want to spend 4 hours a week doing back searches on google news. PressPulp sends across news round ups for all of your brand’s press New product? Funding announcement? You can adjust the timings to mirror your publicity schedules.

Competitor Benchmarking

What are your competitors doing? Which publications or journalists are writing about your industry? PressPulp keeps you up to date on your competitors’ press and illustrates how you fit into the market's share of voice.

Measure your PR success

PR is amazing. It gives you exposure, legitimacy and doesn’t require an ad budget. But it’s very tricky to measure. PressPulp helps you grade your coverage. We breakdown your press and help you score your coverage on the basis of SEO, Brand Messages, Sentiment, Demographics, Reach, Customer Acquisition and other personalised variables contributing to your PR Success.
