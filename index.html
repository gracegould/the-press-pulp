Press Pulp

Press Pulp is a tool to make In-house PR's lives a little bit easier. We collect, analyse and benchmark your brand's coverage.

Keep track of your brands coverage.

We know that it’s a hassle to keep on top of everything that’s written about your brand. Press Pulp sends across digests of all of your brand’s press at a time and frequency best for you. New product? Funding announcement? You can adjust the timings to mirror your publicity schedules.

A metric to measure your PR success.

PR is amazing. It gives you exposure, legitimacy and doesn’t require an ad budget. But it’s very tricky to measure. Press Pulp grades your coverage, giving you an actual PR metric which you can track throughout campaigns. The Press Pulp score is worked out by looking at the sentiment, reach, links, brand message and demographics of an article.

Benchmark your coverage.

Of course you love hearing about what your competitors are doing. Press Pulp keeps you up to date on your competitors’ press. Follow where they’re getting coverage, stay on top of what they’re announcing and see how you all fit into the share of voice.
